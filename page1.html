Particle Identification for LHCb
This project was done for the LHCb research group in order to find whether a new way of measuring a particles speed added new information. The measurement was shown to be successful by appliying a neural network to classify the particles. With the additional information from the new measurement, a significant performance increase could be shown.
PyDrink - How does Alcohol Affect the Human Body
PyDrink finds a personalized correlation between alcohol intake and the feeling of drunkeness (how much a person is affected by the alcohol) for each user, depending on a variety of factors such as weight, height, gender, tiredness, age, etc. I am currently working on turning PyDrink into an application, which would allow it to be used in real life situations and the resulting data can be used to gain new insights into alcohol metabolism.
